TOYOTA MR2 1995 Bathurst

The Toyota MR2 SW20 was one of my dream cars, and so in 2021 I finally bought one. My intention for this car was to be a weekender car that I could also use as a learning platform. I can learn about the different mechanical functions while also making my own projects for modernising the car while maintaining the retro style.

Projects: Sound system, Brakes, Lighting, Simple Mods (Short shifter, bonnet strut, cosmetic), Upcoming
